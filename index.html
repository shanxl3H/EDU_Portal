The purpose of the EDU Portal website is to provide a centralized platform for students, teachers, and parents to access academic resources, manage schedules, communicate, and enhance the overall educational experience.

Students, teachers, and parents can access the EDU portal website by visiting the website's URL on their web browser. The website is accessible through any internet-enabled device, such as a computer, tablet, or smartphone.

Yes, the EDU Portal website is accessible on both desktops and mobile devices. The website is designed to be responsive and adaptive, which means that it can automatically adjust to the screen size and resolution of the device being used to access it.
